# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

## What we collect

- **Account information**: when you sign in with Apple, Google, or an email code, we store your email address to create and identify your account.
- **Your content**: the notes, folders, and images you create. With cloud sync enabled (a Premium feature) they are transmitted over HTTPS and stored in our cloud database (Supabase); otherwise they stay on your device only.
- **Purchase receipts**: when you buy Premium, the App Store / Google Play receipt is sent to Apple / Google for validation. We never see your payment details.

## How we use it

This information is used solely to provide sign-in, cross-device sync, and the Premium unlock. We show **no ads, do no tracking, and never sell your data**.

## Third-party services

The app uses Supabase for accounts and sync, and Apple / Google for sign-in and billing. Their respective privacy policies apply to those services.

## Retention and deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all synced data at any time in-app via *Settings → Delete account*. Deletion is immediate and irreversible. Device-local data can be removed by uninstalling the app.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.
